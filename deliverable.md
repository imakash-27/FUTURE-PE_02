# Deliverable — UGC Ad Content Pack for Prime Relocations

**Business:** Prime Relocations — premium packers & movers, Nellore, Andhra Pradesh
**Author:** Manikantan.P — Prompt Engineering Intern, Future Interns

---

## Persona Constraints (used in every prompt)

- First-person voice only, written as if by a real customer
- No corporate or brand-marketing language ("best in class", "industry-leading", etc.)
- Casual sentence structure, occasional imperfect grammar allowed for authenticity
- Each hook must work as a stand-alone opening line for a 15–30 second video

---

## 6 Scroll-Stopping Hooks

| # | Angle | Hook |
|---|-------|------|
| 1 | Relatable pain | "I moved 3 times in 2 years. This is the only company I trust now." |
| 2 | Cost curiosity | "Everyone told me moving would cost double what it actually did." |
| 3 | Fear reversal | "I was terrified about my grandmother's china surviving the move. It did." |
| 4 | Direct comparison | "I called 4 moving companies. Only one didn't try to upsell me." |
| 5 | Time-pressure | "We had 48 hours to pack an entire office. Here's how it went." |
| 6 | Skeptic turned fan | "I never post about services. But this one earned it." |

---

## Full Ad Scripts (built from strongest 3 hooks)

### Script 1 — Instagram Reel (Hook 1: Relatable pain)
**Hook:** "I moved 3 times in 2 years. This is the only company I trust now."
**Body:** Every other move, something broke or showed up late. This time — nothing. They packed everything, tracked it live, and it all arrived exactly on time.
**CTA:** "Link in bio for a free quote if you're moving in Nellore."
**Caption:** Not sponsored by anyone but honesty. Moving broke me the first two times — this time it didn't. 📦 #MovingDay #Relocation #Nellore

### Script 2 — YouTube Shorts (Hook 3: Fear reversal)
**Hook:** "I was terrified about my grandmother's china surviving the move. It did."
**Body:** Fragile items were wrapped individually, boxed separately, and labeled "handle with care" — and they meant it. Not a single chip.
**CTA:** "Ask about their fragile-item packing when you get your quote."
**Caption:** The one thing I didn't want to risk — handled perfectly. 🏺 #FragileMove #PackersAndMovers

### Script 3 — Facebook (Hook 4: Direct comparison)
**Hook:** "I called 4 moving companies. Only one didn't try to upsell me."
**Body:** No hidden "insurance fees," no last-minute price changes. What they quoted is what I paid.
**CTA:** "Get a transparent quote — no surprises."
**Caption:** Called around so you don't have to. This one's honest. ✅ #TransparentPricing #MovingCompany
